Privacy Policy for Just Noise

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Overview

Just Noise is a simple brown noise generator app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Just Noise:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, photos, or files
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements

How the App Works

Just Noise generates brown noise audio entirely on your device. The app:

  • Runs completely offline
  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Stores no data on your device beyond the app itself

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Audio playback: Required to generate and play brown noise audio
  • Background audio: Allows the app to continue playing when minimized

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of playing audio and nothing else.

Third-Party Services

Just Noise does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Just Noise does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
